About Us

Deepak Transport Agency, Shahjahanpur, is a logistic company, owned by Shri Shailendra Kumar Singh. We have been servicing all across India,specially in states like Kolkata, U.P, Bihar and Gujarat. We have been providing our service from last more than 2 decades.

Services

We provide transportation services such as Full Truck Load, Part load, Rake Load. These services are provided 24*7 all over India keeping in consideration safety and security of the product and everyone involved. Transportation is done in most favourable and efficient way.

Braches

Braches of Deepak Transport are enrooted all over India. We have more than 30 branches spread all over U.P., Bihar, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Gujrat and more. Our branches are well known for their reputation in the market.
